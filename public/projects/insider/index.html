About

Insider is an android app that allows people to play the popular game "Werewolf/Mafia" on their phone. The phone is passed between players throughout the game.

*Note: Insider is getting completely redone to be crossplatform and have "online" play. Which means you can play with anyone, anywhere in the world. This new version is expected to be completed in a few months.
